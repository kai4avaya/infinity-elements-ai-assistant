Altamino Global Conglomerate - Injury and Workplace Safety Procedures

WORKPLACE SAFETY COMMITMENT:
Altamino is committed to maintaining a safe work environment and providing comprehensive care for workplace injuries.

IMMEDIATE RESPONSE PROCEDURES:
1. Life-threatening emergencies: Call 911 immediately
2. Non-emergency injuries: Report to supervisor within 1 hour
3. Near-miss incidents: Report to safety committee within 24 hours
4. Property damage only: Document and report to facilities

MEDICAL CARE PROTOCOL:
- On-site medical: Available at major facilities (8 AM - 6 PM)
- Urgent care: Approved network providers
- Emergency room: Any facility for true emergencies
- Follow-up care: Company-approved specialists

INJURY CLASSIFICATION:
- Minor: First aid treatable, no lost time
- Moderate: Medical attention required, < 3 days lost time
- Serious: Medical treatment required, > 3 days lost time
- Critical: Hospitalization or life-threatening

REPORTING REQUIREMENTS:
- Employee report: Detailed incident form within 24 hours
- Supervisor report: Witness statements and root cause analysis
- Safety committee: Review within 48 hours for serious incidents
- OSHA reporting: As required by law (8-hour for fatalities)

WORKERS' COMPENSATION:
- 100% medical coverage: All work-related injuries
- Wage replacement: 66.67% of average weekly wage
- Temporary disability: Up to 104 weeks
- Permanent disability: Based on medical evaluation
- Vocational rehabilitation: Available for career changes

RETURN-TO-WORK PROGRAM:
- Light duty: Available when medically appropriate
- Modified work: Temporary accommodations available
- Gradual return: Phased return to full duties
- Remote work: Available during recovery period

PREVENTION PROGRAMS:
- Safety training: Monthly mandatory sessions
- Ergonomic assessments: Annual workstation evaluations
- Safety committees: Cross-departmental safety teams
- Hazard identification: Regular workplace inspections

PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE):
- Required PPE: Provided at no cost to employees
- Training: Proper use and maintenance instruction
- Replacement: Worn or damaged PPE replaced immediately
- Specialized PPE: Available for specific job functions

INCIDENT INVESTIGATION:
- Root cause analysis: Required for all incidents
- Corrective actions: Implemented within 30 days
- Follow-up verification: 90-day effectiveness review
- Trend analysis: Quarterly safety performance review

MENTAL HEALTH SUPPORT:
- Critical incident stress: Debriefing within 72 hours
- Counseling services: 12 free sessions per incident
- Peer support: Trained employee volunteers
- Family support: Services available for family members

TRAVEL-RELATED INJURIES:
- Business travel: 100% coverage worldwide
- Emergency evacuation: Medical evacuation insurance
- Local care: Approved international providers
- Translation services: Medical interpretation available

RECORDKEEPING:
- OSHA 300 log: Maintained for all recordable injuries
- Medical records: Confidential, maintained for 5 years
- Incident reports: Archived for 7 years
- Training records: Maintained for employee tenure

EMERGENCY CONTACTS:
- Corporate Safety: 1-800-ALTAMINO ext. 9999
- Medical Emergency: 911 or local emergency number
- Workers' Comp Hotline: 1-800-WORKSAFE
- Crisis Line: 1-877-URGENT-AI (24/7)

FACILITY-SPECIFIC PROCEDURES:
- Manufacturing: Lockout/tagout, machine guarding
- Office: Ergonomics, slip/trip hazards
- Laboratory: Chemical safety, PPE requirements
- Warehouse: Material handling, fall protection

REPORTING TOOLS:
- Online portal: safety.altamino.com
- Mobile app: Altamino Safety (instant reporting)
- Phone hotline: 1-800-REPORT-SAFE
- Paper forms: Available at all safety stations

For detailed safety procedures specific to your department, contact your local Safety Representative or the Corporate Safety Department.
