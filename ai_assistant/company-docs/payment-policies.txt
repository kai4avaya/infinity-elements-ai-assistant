Altamino Global Conglomerate - Payment Policies

PAYMENT PROCESSING OVERVIEW:
Altamino maintains secure, efficient payment systems for all business transactions globally.

EMPLOYEE PAYMENT METHODS:
- Direct Deposit: Preferred method for all employees
- Pay Cards: Available for unbanked employees
- Paper Checks: Available upon request with fees
- International Transfers: Wire transfers for global staff

PAYROLL SCHEDULE:
- Bi-weekly pay: Every other Friday
- Monthly pay: Last business day of month (executive level)
- Bonus payments: Quarterly and annual distributions
- Commission payments: Monthly processing

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT:
- Submission deadline: 15th of following month
- Processing time: 5-7 business days
- Required documentation: Receipts for all expenses > $25
- Mileage reimbursement: Current IRS rate + 20% premium
- Per diem rates: Vary by location and travel duration

CORPORATE PAYMENT CARDS:
- Corporate credit cards: Available for managers and above
- Purchase cards: Departmental spending controls
- Travel cards: Integrated with booking systems
- Virtual cards: One-time use for vendor payments

VENDOR PAYMENTS:
- Net 30 terms: Standard for most vendors
- Early payment discounts: 2% if paid in 10 days
- International payments: Wire transfer preferred
- Currency conversion: Competitive rates through banking partners

INVOICE PROCESSING:
- Electronic invoicing: Preferred method (EDI/ERP integration)
- Approval workflows: Multi-level based on amount
- Payment scheduling: Optimized for cash flow
- Audit trail: Complete documentation retained

INTERNATIONAL PAYMENTS:
- SWIFT transfers: For international vendors
- Currency hedging: Risk management program
- Local banking partners: 150+ countries covered
- Compliance screening: AML and sanctions checks

PAYMENT SECURITY:
- Two-factor authentication: Required for all payments > $1,000
- Encryption: End-to-end encryption for all transactions
- Fraud monitoring: Real-time transaction analysis
- Insurance coverage: Payment fraud insurance up to $10M

TAX COMPLIANCE:
- 1099 reporting: All contractor payments
- International tax treaties: Optimized for global operations
- Withholding requirements: Country-specific compliance
- Tax documentation: W-9, W-8BEN forms maintained

EMERGENCY PAYMENTS:
- Emergency payroll: Same-day processing available
- Disaster relief: Special payment programs
- Hardship advances: Available with manager approval
- International emergencies: Embassy coordination support

PAYMENT DISPUTES:
- Resolution timeline: 30 business days maximum
- Escalation process: Multi-level review available
- Legal support: In-house counsel for complex disputes
- Arbitration: Alternative dispute resolution available
